## 🏫 Overview

Smart Life Solutions (SLS) is an educational and mentorship organization that equips students and young adults with practical life skills, leadership development, and spiritual growth opportunities.  

This website serves as a central platform for:
- Showcasing programs and services  
- Sharing student success stories through the blog  
- Displaying project galleries and events  
- Providing an easy contact point for partnerships and memberships  

The site is **static**, responsive, and works **completely offline** — no database or backend is required.
